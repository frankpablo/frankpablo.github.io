Graduation 2021

Dear Computer Science Class of 2021:

CONGRATULATIONS !! This is an exciting time for all of you, as you prepare for your graduation and the end of undergraduate classes forever! Today is the day to celebrate the results of your hard work and your achievements. So please take the time to soak it all in and celebrate your graduation!

An annual highlight for faculty is the informal reception before the SEAS graduation where we get a chance to celebrate with you and your families. Although we are unable to do that in person this year, the CS faculty would like to host an event on Friday, May 14th, from 2:00--3:30pm for our students and their families before the SEAS celebration on Friday and hope that you will drop in. We will have several faculty waiting to meet you in their "rooms" from 2pm--3pm, and then we will meet as a department from 3pm--3:30pm. We look forward to meeting you and your family at both. 

  • Event Zoom Room: https://gwu-edu.zoom.us/j/98378476237

Once in, we'll have you roam around the breakout rooms where you can chat with the faculty.
Once started, please message faculty/staff directly. They'll keep track of the messages and come get you from their breakout rooms.

See You all there!!
