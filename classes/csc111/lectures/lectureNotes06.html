"Calling different players"

Let's try grouping repeated sections and assigning that section to a function:

That would be like giving a sequence of notes to one musician and another sequence to another musician.

The conductor (control flow) would then call on (invoke) the correct section at the correct time:

This might look, in code, like this: 
def tripleA():
  print('A\nA\nA')

def FCA():
  print('F\nC\nA')

def tripleE():
  print('E\nE\nE')

def FCG():
  print('F\nC\nG')  

tripleA()
FCA()
FCA()
print()
tripleE()
FCG()
FCA()
